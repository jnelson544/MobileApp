Rescue Pup

Helping you find your perfect furry friend!

This app is for people looking to adopt their best friends. They can go on to the site and view dogs in their area. The site provides pictures, name, age, and breed information as well as their location.
If someone is interested in the pup they can add that dog to their favorites list. The app will allow them to add and remove dogs from their favorites list. There is also a section which will allow
current dog owners to list their dogs for adoption which will connect those looking to adopt to a wider net of people looking to find a good home for their pup.
